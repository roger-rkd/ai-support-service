Billing and Payment Information

Subscription Plans:
We offer three subscription tiers:
- Basic Plan: $9.99/month - Essential features for individuals
- Pro Plan: $29.99/month - Advanced features for professionals
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing - Full features with dedicated support

Payment Methods:
We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit Cards
- PayPal
- Bank Transfer (for Enterprise plans)

Billing Cycle:
- Monthly subscriptions are billed on the same day each month
- Annual subscriptions save 20% and are billed once per year
- You can change your billing cycle anytime in your account settings

Updating Payment Information:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods
3. Click "Add Payment Method" or "Update" next to existing method
4. Enter your new payment information
5. Click "Save"

Viewing Invoices:
- All invoices are available in Settings > Billing > Invoice History
- You can download invoices as PDF files
- Invoices are also emailed to your registered email address

Canceling Subscription:
1. Go to Settings > Billing > Subscription
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Select a reason for cancellation (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period

Refund Policy:
- We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- To request a refund, contact our support team with your order number
- Annual subscriptions are prorated if canceled mid-year

Failed Payment:
If a payment fails:
- We'll automatically retry after 3 days
- You'll receive email notifications
- Your account will be suspended after 7 days of failed payment
- Update your payment method to restore access
