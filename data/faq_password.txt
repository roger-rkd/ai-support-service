How to Reset Your Password

If you've forgotten your password or need to reset it for security reasons, follow these steps:

1. Go to the login page
2. Click on "Forgot Password?" link below the login form
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email inbox for a password reset link (it may take a few minutes)
5. Click the link in the email (valid for 24 hours)
6. Create a new strong password (minimum 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters)
7. Confirm your new password
8. Click "Reset Password" button

If you don't receive the email:
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you're using the correct email address
- Wait 5 minutes and try requesting again
- Contact support if the issue persists

Password Requirements:
- Minimum 8 characters long
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Cannot be the same as your last 3 passwords
