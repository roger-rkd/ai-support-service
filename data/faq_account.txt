Account Management Guide

Creating a New Account:
1. Visit our registration page at www.example.com/register
2. Fill in your personal information (name, email, phone number)
3. Choose a strong password
4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. Complete your profile setup

Updating Account Information:
- Log in to your account
- Navigate to Settings > Profile
- Edit the information you want to change
- Click "Save Changes"
- Some changes may require email verification

Account Verification:
- All new accounts must verify their email address
- Verification emails are sent immediately after registration
- The verification link expires after 48 hours
- You can request a new verification email from your account settings

Closing Your Account:
If you wish to close your account permanently:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings > Account > Close Account
3. Read the information about account closure
4. Enter your password to confirm
5. Click "Permanently Close Account"

Note: Account closure is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted after 30 days.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):
- We highly recommend enabling 2FA for additional security
- Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication
- Choose your preferred method (SMS or Authenticator App)
- Follow the setup instructions
- Keep your backup codes in a safe place
