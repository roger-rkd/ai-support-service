Account Management Guide\n\nCreating a New Account:\n1. Visit our registration page at www.example.com/register\n2. Fill in your personal information (name, email, phone number)\n3. Choose a strong password\n4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox\n5. Complete your profile setup\n\nUpdating Account Information:\n- Log in to your account\n- Navigate to Settings > Profile\n- Edit the information you want to change\n- Click "Save Changes"\n- Some changes may require email verification\n\nAccount Verification:\n- All new accounts must verify their email address\n- Verification emails are sent immediately after registration\n- The verification link expires after 48 hours\n- You can request a new verification email from your account settings\n\nClosing Your Account:\nIf you wish to close your account permanently:\n1. Log in to your account\n2. Go to Settings > Account > Close Account\n3. Read the information about account closure\n4. Enter your password to confirm\n5. Click "Permanently Close Account"\n\nNote: Account closure is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted after 30 days.\n\nTwo-Factor Authentication (2FA):\n- We highly recommend enabling 2FA for additional security\n- Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication\n- Choose your preferred method (SMS or Authenticator App)\n- Follow the setup instructions\n- Keep your backup codes in a safe place
Billing and Payment Information\n\nSubscription Plans:\nWe offer three subscription tiers:\n- Basic Plan: $9.99/month - Essential features for individuals\n- Pro Plan: $29.99/month - Advanced features for professionals\n- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing - Full features with dedicated support\n\nPayment Methods:\nWe accept the following payment methods:\n- Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)\n- Debit Cards\n- PayPal\n- Bank Transfer (for Enterprise plans)\n\nBilling Cycle:\n- Monthly subscriptions are billed on the same day each month\n- Annual subscriptions save 20% and are billed once per year\n- You can change your billing cycle anytime in your account settings\n\nUpdating Payment Information:\n1. Log in to your account\n2. Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods\n3. Click "Add Payment Method" or "Update" next to existing method\n4. Enter your new payment information\n5. Click "Save"\n\nViewing Invoices:\n- All invoices are available in Settings > Billing > Invoice History\n- You can download invoices as PDF files\n- Invoices are also emailed to your registered email address\n\nCanceling Subscription:\n1. Go to Settings > Billing > Subscription\n2. Click "Cancel Subscription"\n3. Select a reason for cancellation (optional)\n4. Confirm cancellation\n5. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period\n\nRefund Policy:\n- We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions\n- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days\n- To request a refund, contact our support team with your order number\n- Annual subscriptions are prorated if canceled mid-year\n\nFailed Payment:\nIf a payment fails:\n- We'll automatically retry after 3 days\n- You'll receive email notifications\n- Your account will be suspended after 7 days of failed payment\n- Update your payment method to restore access
How to Reset Your Password\n\nIf you've forgotten your password or need to reset it for security reasons, follow these steps:\n\n1. Go to the login page\n2. Click on "Forgot Password?" link below the login form\n3. Enter your registered email address\n4. Check your email inbox for a password reset link (it may take a few minutes)\n5. Click the link in the email (valid for 24 hours)\n6. Create a new strong password (minimum 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters)\n7. Confirm your new password\n8. Click "Reset Password" button\n\nIf you don't receive the email:\n- Check your spam/junk folder\n- Verify you're using the correct email address\n- Wait 5 minutes and try requesting again\n- Contact support if the issue persists\n\nPassword Requirements:\n- Minimum 8 characters long\n- At least one uppercase letter\n- At least one lowercase letter\n- At least one number\n- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)\n- Cannot be the same as your last 3 passwords
Technical Support and Troubleshooting\n\nBrowser Compatibility:\nOur service works best with the latest versions of:\n- Google Chrome (recommended)\n- Mozilla Firefox\n- Safari\n- Microsoft Edge\n\nMinimum system requirements:\n- Internet connection: 5 Mbps or faster\n- Screen resolution: 1280x720 or higher\n- JavaScript must be enabled\n- Cookies must be enabled\n\nCommon Issues and Solutions:\n\n1. Can't log in:\n   - Clear your browser cache and cookies\n   - Try using incognito/private mode\n   - Ensure Caps Lock is off\n   - Reset your password if needed\n   - Try a different browser\n\n2. Page not loading or slow performance:\n   - Check your internet connection\n   - Clear browser cache\n   - Disable browser extensions temporarily\n   - Try a different network\n   - Check our status page for any ongoing issues\n\n3. Features not working:\n   - Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)\n   - Clear cache and cookies\n   - Update your browser to the latest version\n   - Disable ad blockers for our site\n   - Check if JavaScript is enabled\n\n4. File upload issues:\n   - Check file size (maximum 50MB per file)\n   - Supported formats: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, JPG, PNG\n   - Ensure stable internet connection\n   - Try uploading one file at a time\n   - Use a wired connection if possible\n\n5. Mobile app issues:\n   - Update the app to the latest version\n   - Clear app cache (Settings > Apps > Our App > Clear Cache)\n   - Restart your device\n   - Uninstall and reinstall the app\n   - Check for OS updates\n\nAPI Access:\n- API documentation: docs.example.com/api\n- Rate limits: 1000 requests per hour for Pro plans\n- Authentication: OAuth 2.0 or API keys\n- Contact us for increased rate limits\n\nSecurity Best Practices:\n- Use a strong, unique password\n- Enable two-factor authentication\n- Don't share your account credentials\n- Log out when using shared computers\n- Keep your software up to date\n- Be cautious of phishing emails\n\nData Export:\n- You can export your data anytime\n- Go to Settings > Data > Export Data\n- Choose format (JSON, CSV, or XML)\n- Download will be ready within 24 hours\n- Link is valid for 7 days\n\nContact Technical Support:\n- Email: support@example.com\n- Live chat: Available Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 6 PM EST\n- Response time: Within 24 hours\n- Include your account email and description of the issue
